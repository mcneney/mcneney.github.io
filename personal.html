This photo of me was taken by Jinko Graham (pictured below) while we were skiing in Garibaldi Provincial Park near Squamish, BC. (I'm originally from Squamish.)

I finished my Ph.D. in 1998 in the Department of Biostatistics at the University of Washington, Seattle, Washington under the supervision of Jon Wellner in the UW Statistics Department. I then did a Postdoc with Butch Tsiatis in the Department of Statisitcs at NC State University and at the National Institute of Statistical Sciences. Since September 1999 I have been an Assistant Professor in the Department of Statistics and Actuarial Sciences at Simon Fraser University in Burnaby, BC.
